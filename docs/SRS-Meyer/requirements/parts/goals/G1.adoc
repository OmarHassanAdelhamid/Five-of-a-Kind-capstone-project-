At McMaster University, the Computing and Software Department working on robotics and soft robotics has faced challenges in efficiently designing and modifying voxel-based 3D-printed components. While 3D printers and traditional CAD software are used, existing tools are time-consuming and inflexible, making voxel-level edits difficult and lengthy. By developing a tailored voxel design tool, Team Five of a Kind aims to provide a cost-effective and user-friendly solution that enables researchers to efficiently edit and voxel properties layer by layer.
